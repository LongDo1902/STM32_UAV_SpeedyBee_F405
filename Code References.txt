SpeedyBee F405 V4 uses:
STM32F405RGT6
ICM42688P: SCK (PA5), MISO (PA6), MOSI (PA7), CS (PA4)
DPS310: SCL (PB8), SDA (PB9)
ADC Channel 1: PC0
ADC Channel 2: PC1
ADC Channel 3: PC5
LED1: PC13 Blue
